Prepare yourself for The Mile. Your game will save automatically at every dinging glowing square.

The Discotheque at Fassilia V is widely considered to be the most dangerous in the universe. The dance floor, which covers the entire surface of the planet, was constructed as a conservation effort, to encourage breeding among the few remaining native Fassilions.

Due to the extinction of the Fassilions shortly thereafter in a series of disco-related accidents, the hall was opened to any citizen of the galaxy — any citizen willing, either, to obey its strict aesthetic rules, or to accept the consequences.

Adrenaline junkies from across the universe trickle into the Fassilia system in ones or twos; they land and trek, step-by-step across the silent, bright, and barren corridors. It is considered a badge of honor to have "done the mile" on Fassilia V and lived.

Among those who survive, rumors fly of shadowy figures prowling the horizon — a twisted few who stay longer, in whom the rules of the floor become so ingrained that they never leave. This, when discussed in hushed tones in dim bars, is called "Pulling a Renshaw." No one knows why.
